Professional Conduct Standards

Professional Language

Always communicate clearly and respectfully. Avoid slang, sarcasm, or dismissive responses. Staff represent the organization at all times.

Respectful Tone

Remain calm with frustrated users. Never argue. Focus on solutions and keep interactions constructive.

Response Quality

Provide complete answers. Do not give one-word replies. Explain steps clearly so users understand what to do.

Authority & Responsibility

Do not misuse permissions. Moderation tools exist to protect the community, not for personal disputes.

Confidentiality

Never share private staff discussions, reports, or internal decisions with regular members.

Reliability

Be dependable. If you claim a ticket or task, complete it fully instead of leaving it for others.

Ticket System Mastery

General Tickets

Support inquiries, assistance requests, and reports. Handle politely and escalate serious issues properly.

Roster Tickets

Applications to join Surge Esports. Must follow structured onboarding and verification steps.

Protocol: Begin with a professional greeting and age verification. Maintain detailed records and guide users clearly through next steps.

Important: Tickets labeled ticket-staff are for Staff Department members or higher only. Many new staff forget this rule.

Ticket Handling Guidelines

Here is how you should proceed in tickets:

Remember to be professional at all times. Refrain from using informal responses like "Yo", "Hi", or "Bro". Always start with a friendly and professional greeting.

Professional Greetings

Hello, how may I help you?
Hello, what can I assist you with today?

Role Inquiry

What role are you applying for today?
What are you applying for here?

Age Verification

May I ask how old you are?
What is your current age?

Blacklist Format

User: Username of the person
User ID: Discord ID
Reason: Underage
